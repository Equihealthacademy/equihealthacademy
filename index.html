Who We Are

EquiHealth Academy is a neutral, non-profit institution established for public benefit. We serve as a vital bridge between training and service.

Rooted in the belief that health is broader than disease treatment alone—encompassing education, social support, environmental conditions, water and sanitation, and dignity.

Our activities include seminars, workshops, mentorship programmes, community outreach, philanthropy, and responsible AI education.

Our Vision

A generation of skilled, socially responsible young leaders using knowledge, technology, and compassion to improve health and education.

Our Mission

To equip young people with practical skills, ethical leadership, and community service experience that improve public health.
